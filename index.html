About Me

About Me

My name is Max Einspinner. I live in Mona Vale on Sydney’s Northern Beaches and enjoy spending time outdoors, especially surfing, camping, and exploring new places. I am currently studying at the Australian Catholic University, undertaking a double degree in Secondary Education and Design and Technology.

My Passion for Teaching

My passion for teaching comes from my own experiences in school. I wasn’t a high-achieving student, but I had teachers who believed in me. Their encouragement shaped my approach to education—building confidence and helping students realise their strengths.

Teaching Practice

Core Beliefs

I believe in creating inclusive, hands-on learning environments where every student has the opportunity to discover their potential and take pride in their achievements. My teaching is grounded in encouragement, accessibility, and growth.

My Teaching Philosophy

Central to my philosophy is the belief that education should inspire students and provide opportunities for every learner to realise their potential. I integrate hands-on, project-based learning that fosters creativity and problem-solving.
